Pearl

Polar bears evolved from brown to white for camouflage purposes.

  • Scientific Name: Ursus maritimus
  • Average Length: 7ft to 9ft 10 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 25 years
  • Habitat: Artic Ice Shelf

"Male polar bears have longer haris on their front legs for mating. The hair and blubber on polar bears are 10 inches thick. Polar bears can swim over 200 miles. Polar bears climb cliffs to eat bird chicks and eggs from nest. Polar bears feed on ringed seals and bearded seals, they are also know for eating walrus, beluga whales and rarely vegetation. They will travel great distances in search of prey. Polar bears have the cutest babies!"
